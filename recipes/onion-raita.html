Onion Raita

Onion raita is a refreshing and cooling Indian side dish made with yogurt, onions, and a blend of herbs and spices, commonly served with biryanis, pulaos, and spicy curries to balance heat.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt: Use fresh, plain, full-fat yogurt for creaminess.
  • Onions: Red onions are preferred for their mild flavor.
  • Herbs: Cilantro (coriander leaves) and sometimes mint.
  • Spices: Cumin powder, salt, black salt (kala namak), and green chilies for heat.
  • Optional additions include sugar, vinegar, or roasted cumin for extra flavor.

Step-by-step

  1. Prepare Onions: Finely chop or slice onions. Some recipes call for soaking them in vinegar or salt to reduce sharpness.
  2. Whisk Yogurt: Beat yogurt until smooth, then mix in salt, spices, and herbs.
  3. Combine: Add onions and any optional ingredients. Mix gently.
  4. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to enhance flavors.
